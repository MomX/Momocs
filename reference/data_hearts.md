# Data: Outline coordinates of hand-drawn hearts

Data: Outline coordinates of hand-drawn hearts

## Format

A [Out](http://momx.github.io/Momocs/reference/Out.md) object with the
outline coordinates of 240 hand-drawn hearts by 8 different persons,
with 4 landmarks.

## Source

We thank the fellows of the Ecology Department of the French Institute
of Pondicherry that drawn the hearts, that then have been smoothed,
scaled, centered, and downsampled to 80 coordinates per outline.
