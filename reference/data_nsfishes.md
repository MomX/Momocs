# Data: Outline coordinates of North Sea fishes

Data: Outline coordinates of North Sea fishes

## Format

A [Out](http://momx.github.io/Momocs/reference/Out.md) object containing
the outlines coordinates for 218 fishes from the North Sea along with
taxonomical cofactors.

## Source

Caillon F, Frelat R, Mollmann C, Bonhomme V (submitted)
