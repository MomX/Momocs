# Momocs

The goal of Momocs is to provide a complete, convenient, reproducible
and open-source toolkit for 2D morphometrics. It includes most common 2D
morphometrics approaches on outlines, open outlines, configurations of
landmarks, traditional morphometrics, and facilities for data
preparation, manipulation and visualization with a consistent grammar
throughout. It allows reproducible, complex morphometric analyses and
other morphometrics approaches should be easy to plug in, or develop
from, on top of this canvas.
