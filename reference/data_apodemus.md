# Data: Outline coordinates of Apodemus (wood mouse) mandibles

Data: Outline coordinates of Apodemus (wood mouse) mandibles

## Format

A [Out](http://momx.github.io/Momocs/reference/Out.md) object 64
coordinates of 30 wood molar outlines.

## Source

Renaud S, Pale JRM, Michaux JR (2003): Adaptive latitudinal trends in
the mandible shape of *Apodemus* wood mice. *Journal of Biogeography*
30:1617-1628. see
`https://onlinelibrary.wiley.com/doi/full/10.1046/j.1365-2699.2003.00932.x`
