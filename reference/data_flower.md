# Data: Measurement of iris flowers

Data: Measurement of iris flowers

## Format

A `TraCoe` object with 150 measurements of 4 variables (petal + sepal) x
(length x width) on 3 species of iris. This dataset is the classical
[iris](https://rdrr.io/r/datasets/iris.html) formatted for Momocs.

## Source

see [iris](https://rdrr.io/r/datasets/iris.html)
