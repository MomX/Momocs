# Data: Landmark and semilandmark coordinates on cereal glumes

Data: Landmark and semilandmark coordinates on cereal glumes

## Format

An [Ldk](http://momx.github.io/Momocs/reference/Ldk.md) object with 21
configurations of landmarks and semi-landmarks (4 partitions) sampled on
cereal glumes

## Source

Research support was provided by the European Research Council
(Evolutionary Origins of Agriculture (grant no. 269830-EOA) PI: Glynis
Jones, Dept of Archaeology, Sheffield, UK. Data collected by Emily
Forster.
