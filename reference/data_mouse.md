# Data: Outline coordinates of mouse molars

Data: Outline coordinates of mouse molars

## Format

A [Out](http://momx.github.io/Momocs/reference/Out.md) object 64
coordinates of 30 wood molar outlines.

## Source

Renaud S, Dufour AB, Hardouin EA, Ledevin R, Auffray JC (2015): Once
upon multivariate analyses: When they tell several stories about
biological evolution. *PLoS One* 10:1-18
<https://journals.plos.org/plosone/article?id=10.1371/journal.pone.0132801>
