# Data: Outline coordinates from an experimental charring on cereal grains

Data: Outline coordinates from an experimental charring on cereal grains

## Format

An [Out](http://momx.github.io/Momocs/reference/Out.md) object with 18
grains, 3 views on each, for 2 cereal species, charred at different
temperatures for 6 hours (0C (no charring), 230C and 260C).

## Source

Research support was provided by the European Research Council
(Evolutionary Origins of Agriculture (grant no. 269830-EOA) PI: Glynis
Jones, Dept of Archaeology, Sheffield, UK. Data collected by Emily
Forster.
